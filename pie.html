Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie
30 Min Prep-Time
10 Min-CookTime
40 Min-Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Cup white sugar
  • 2
    tbspTableSpoon
    All Purpose Flour
  • 3
    tbspTableSpoon
    Cornstarch
  • 1/4
    tspTeaSpoon
    Salt
  • 1 1/2 Cups Water
  • 2 Lemons Juiced and zested
  • 2
    tbspTableSpoon
    Butter
  • 4 eggs yolk,beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6
    tbspTableSpoon
    White sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven:Preheat Oven to 350 degree F (175 degree C
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour, Cornstarch and Salt
    • Stir in Water,Lemon juice and lemon zest
    • Cook over medium-high heat stiring frequently,until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter
    • Place egg yolk in a small bowl and gratually Whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture
    • Bring to a boil a continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
    • Remove from heat
    • Pour filling into baked pastry shell
  3. Make Meringue: In a larg glass or metal bowl...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy
    • Add sugar gratually and continue to whip until stiff peaks form
    • Spread Meringue over pie,Sealing the edges at the crust
  4. Bake: Beake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until Meringue is golden brown
